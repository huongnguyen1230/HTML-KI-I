Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gentle mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. using a spatula fold the eggs of the pan to the center every 20 seconds
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat
